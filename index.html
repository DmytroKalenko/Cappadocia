PRIVATE HOT AIR BALLOON RIDE IN CAPPADOCIA

Why a private hot air balloon ride? Because hot air ballooning is a lifetime experience we highly recommend spending more but get the best possible experience! Over the years due to the high demand for hot air ballooning in Cappadocia, and the limited balloon rides organized by the companies have changed the basket sizes overall.

HORSE BACK RIDING TOUR

You can ride through the vineyards in the impressive Cappadocia valleys with your horse and take amazing photos throughout the day. You don't have to be a rider, maybe could be even riding for the first time. Our expert guides accompany you for our well trained calm horses to find the mysterious valleys of Cappadocia.

You can join to 1 hour and 2 hours tours, in addition to sunrise and especially famous sunset tours.

KING'S VALLEY MORNING TOUR

A tractor comes to pick you up for an adventure on its wagon! Sultan Cave Suites guests are welcome to join an activitiy organized only for Kelebek Group guests!

8:30 am is when the adventure begins. You will be riding to the most beautiful farm in Cappadocia where you will experience the traditional agricultural teqniques and taste the local dishes of Anatolia.

The farm used to be Mr. Ali Yavuz's family garden where he grew organic fruits and vegetables since his child hood.

SMALL GROUP GREEN TOUR

Next, we will drive to the magnificent Ilhara Valley, where we will walk alongside the Melendiz River for about 1 hour which winds its way through the unique home to rock carved churches and underground dwellings. With nearly 100 in total, they line both sides of the gorge. After hiking, lunch will be served in a restaurant on river in the village of Belisirma.

PRIVATE TOUR

Our travel Agency Goreme Kelebek Travel can arrange a private tour in Cappadocia that is tailored to your interests.

Private tours include transportation, experienced tour guide, meal, and museum entry tickets. You will have a chance to visit Goreme or Zelve Open-Air Museums, Derinkuyu or Kaymakli Underground City, Love Valley, Uchisar, Pigeon Valley, sunset point, Ortahisar Village Panorama, and the other historical sights.

DELUXE BALLON FLIGHT

Ballooning is a very unique activity that your pilot does not have any engine, break, or direction. The pilot uses the burners to heat to the balloon to go up and catch different wind directions. The beautiful landscape with fairy chimneys and caves in Cappadocia has a radius of about 3km. So it is important to have enough time to see around and catch perfect shots.

Basket sizes are 2 to 32 people in Cappadocia! While we highly avoid you to join 32 people baskets we encourage baskets up to 16 people for the most memorable balloon ride!

STANDARD BALLOON RIDES IN CAPPADOCIA!

Standard Balloon Rides in Cappadocia offered by Kelebek Travel are up to 20 people in one Basket. The basket sizes in the region go up to 32 people per basket but some of the smaller hot air ballooning operators like Turquaz Balloons have a higher standard and provides more for the price-quality wise.

JOIN TURKISH NIGHT

Turkish night is an activity where you get to see a quick look of all the traditional and folk dances of Turkey.

This show where you can watch the best examples of traditional Turkish folk dance from professional dancers will last about 2 hours. At the end of the night, your driver will take you back to your hotel.

DERVISH SHOW

Mevlana Celaleddin Rum-i’s teachings and one of the meditations of the Islamic religion is a ceremony that shows the devotees devote themselves to God for centuries. The Dervishes perform with a deep movement of Sufism under high concentration.

ATV TOUR

This tour is recommended for those who love adrenaline. As a standard tour is 2 hours that you can join at any hour of the day but sunset tour more popular among others. A professional guide will take you to Love Valley, Cavusin village, Meskender Valley, Swords Valley and Red Valleys during the tour.

JEEP SAFARI

​For those who want to explore Cappadocia away from the crowds and in a different way, you can enjoy the impressive nature with our jeep safari tour that you can do along the footpaths of the valleys and the village vineyards scattered between them. Our jeep safari tour which is one of the most exciting activities of Cappadocia is taken to the valleys where you can not go with your own vehicles or tour vehicles.

COCING CLASS

We will pick you up from your hotel at 10:00 and we will drive to a local market in Cappadocia for ingredient shopping. Then, we will drive to one of a kind unique King's Valley of Sultan Cave for a memorable cooking experience in the middle of untouched nature. Offered by the local village women, our cooking class is an opportunity to learn how to make the basic Cappadocian meals including soup, two dishes (dolma and sarma), seaspnal main course(eg.

HIKING TOUR

Hiking Tours is available for those who has time to discover deep inside the valleys of Cappadocia. We can arrange a private tour guide for your hiking tour and you will visit the most unique parts of Cappadocia. Hiking can be as difficult or long as you wish. Your guide will take you to the most scenic parts of the valleys. You will be picked up from the hotel and dropped back to the hotel.

RED TOUR

Next stop will be Zelve Open Air Museum, which once housed one of the largest communities in the region which is an amazing cave town, honeycombed with dwellings, religious and secular chambers. We will also see the Church with Deer, the Church with Grapes, the Church with Fishes. Next, we will stop at Devrent's Valley also known as Imaginary Valley to take photos. Then, we will drive to a unique location called King's Valley in Goreme for home made lunch prepared with organic ingredients. After lunch we will see a kick-wheel pottery demonstration in Avanos and our guests are welcome to try this tradition.

Hotel
Sultan Cave Suites

At this unique hotel located on the top of Aydinli Hill, you can book rock-cut rooms furnished with antique furniture from all over Turkey. The hotel's sun terrace offers beautiful panoramic views of the Goreme National Park.

Most of the rooms at Sultan Cave Suites are carved into the rock to keep you cool and peaceful. All rooms are equipped with a seating area and a modern bathroom. Most have a private terrace or balcony.

Cave Suites Sultan has a gourmet restaurant serving specialties from the Cappadocia region. After dinner, head out onto the terrace to enjoy a drink and watch the sun set over the village.
