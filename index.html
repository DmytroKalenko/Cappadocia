Hotel
Sultan Cave Suites

At this unique hotel located on the top of Aydinli Hill, you can book rock-cut rooms furnished with antique furniture from all over Turkey. The hotel's sun terrace offers beautiful panoramic views of the Goreme National Park.

Most of the rooms at Sultan Cave Suites are carved into the rock to keep you cool and peaceful. All rooms are equipped with a seating area and a modern bathroom. Most have a private terrace or balcony.

Cave Suites Sultan has a gourmet restaurant serving specialties from the Cappadocia region. After dinner, head out onto the terrace to enjoy a drink and watch the sun set over the village.
